Virtual Cell Line

A sandbox for hypothesis-driven discovery: simulate gene expression perturbations and observe their effects on cellular systems.

Cell Selection & Filtering

Select cells based on their characteristics. Filter by gene expression, tissue, cell type, inflammation status, and more, to focus on your area of interest.

Gene Perturbation Simulation

Choose a gene to perturb and set a target expression level. The model simulates how changes in one gene's expression affect the entire cellular system over time.

Interactive Visualizations

Explore results through intuitive visualizations, including 2d cell state projections and dynamic gene expression plots. Easily identify the most impacted genes and track expression changes over time.

Customizable Simulation Parameters

Fine-tune your simulations with advanced options, allowing for precise control over the perturbation process.
